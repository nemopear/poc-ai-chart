# Data Schema Definitions

## Revenue Metrics
- **revenue**: Total income from product sales and services
- **gross_revenue**: Revenue before deductions
- **net_revenue**: Revenue after returns and discounts

## Cost Metrics
- **cost**: Total operational expenses
- **cost_of_goods_sold (COGS)**: Direct costs of producing goods
- **operating_expenses**: Day-to-day business running costs

## Profit Metrics
- **gross_profit**: gross_revenue - cost_of_goods_sold
- **net_profit**: gross_profit - operating_expenses
- **profit_margin**: (net_profit / revenue) * 100

## Time-Based Metrics
- **monthly_revenue**: Revenue aggregated by month
- **quarterly_revenue**: Revenue aggregated by quarter
- **year_to_date**: Cumulative revenue from start of year

## Customer Metrics
- **customer_count**: Number of unique customers
- **new_customers**: Customers acquired in period
- **churned_customers**: Customers lost in period
- **customer_lifetime_value**: Average revenue per customer

## Product Metrics
- **product_sales**: Revenue from product sales
- **service_revenue**: Revenue from services
- **units_sold**: Quantity of products sold
